Publications

Approximate Orthogonal Gradient Descent for the Continual Reinforcement Learning of Robot Control
Sanghun Park, SooJean Han
CKAIA 2025
Developed a computationally efficient approximation of orthogonal gradient descent for robot control tasks. The method uses cosine similarity between new task gradients and sampled past gradients from a memory buffer to measure confidence in orthogonality. By projecting onto the orthogonal direction of sampled gradients and weighting by cosine similarity, the approach efficiently approximates orthogonal gradient descent. Validated on Mujoco Reacher and Ant environments.
